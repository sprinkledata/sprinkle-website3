Questions

Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Feel free to contact us

Can I get data delivered from Sprinkle to other tools?

Sprinkle allows you to deliver your data as CSV & PDF directly via email & slack. Additionally, you can use Sprinkle APIs allowing you to programmatically access and integrate data delivery features into custom workflows or applications..

Can I share reports & dashboards with people who don't have Sprinkle accounts?

You can embed Sprinkle dashboards in your application or share the embedded link  with people who don’t have a sprinkle account allowing them to run & view the dashboard.

Do I need to manage report exports individually?

No! Sprinkle offers a centralised location to manage all your data delivery needs. You can manage and track delivery history  for all your exports under the “Schedule” module of Sprinkle, saving you valuable time and effort.
