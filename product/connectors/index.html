Questions

Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Feel free to contact us

How can I schedule data refreshes?

Sprinkle allows you to set up automated data refreshes to ensure your data stays up-to-date. You can choose the frequency (e.g., daily, weekly) for these refreshes.

What does "incremental" and "snapshot" mean in data ingestion?

Sprinkle offers two data ingestion methods:
‍
. Incremental: Fetches new data since the last successful run, avoiding duplicate entries.
. Snapshot: Pulls all data from the start date you specify, ideal for initial data loads.

How long does it take to set up a data connection?

Setting up connections with pre-built connectors is very quick and can be done in just a few clicks.

Is my data secure when ingesting it into Sprinkle?

Sprinkle prioritizes data security. They utilize industry-standard security practices to protect your data during ingestion and storage.

What is data ingestion, and why is it important?

Data ingestion is the process of extracting data from various sources and loading it into a central location for analysis.
‍
With Sprinkle you to easily bring your data together into your data warehouse.  This enables you to have a unified platform for analyzing all your data, leading to better insights and data-driven decisions.
