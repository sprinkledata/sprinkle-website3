What types of visualizations can I use in Sprinkle?

Sprinkle offers a wide range of visualisation options, including:Bar charts: Effective for comparing different categories of data.
‍
Line charts: Ideal for showing trends and changes over time.
Pie charts: Useful for illustrating the composition of a whole.
Scatter plots: Helpful for identifying relationships between two variables.
Maps: Allow you to visualise data geographically and many more.

How much can i customise my dashboards?

Sprinkle offers a user-friendly interface for customising dashboards.You can drag-and-drop different reports and visualisations, arrange them to your preference, and apply filters to focus on specific aspects of your data.

Can I customise the appearance of my visualisations?

Yes, Sprinkle offers customisation options for visualisations, allowing you to adjust colours, fonts, and layouts to match your preferences and branding.

Can I schedule report delivery?

Yes, Sprinkle allows you to schedule reports at specific intervals (e.g., daily, weekly) and deliver them to your inbox or designated recipients.
