Questions

Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Feel free to contact us

Can I change my plan or purchase add-ons later?

Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your plan, add features, or switch your billing cycle directly within the Sprinkle app.

Any price adjustments will be automatically calculated and prorated accordingly.

My main focus is embedded analytics. How does pricing work?

Sprinkle pricing primarily focuses on your overall data usage and team size, regardless of whether you leverage embedded dashboards or standard reporting features.

However, if you have specific requirements for large-scale embedded deployments, feel free to contact us to discuss custom options.

Do you offer enterprise plans for larger organizations?

Yes, Sprinkle offers customizable enterprise plans designed to cater to the specific needs of larger teams and organizations.

These plans can include:
. Increased data storage capacity to accommodate larger datasets.
. Advanced security features for enhanced data governance and   compliance.
. Dedicated customer support for priority assistance and account   management.

contact us to discuss your specific requirements and receive a personalized quote.

Why might prices differ between Sprinkle's on-premise and cloud-based solutions?

Sprinkle's cloud-based solution eliminates the need for your organization to manage hardware and maintain infrastructure, potentially leading to lower total cost of ownership compared to an on-premise deployment.

However, the initial setup costs for the cloud solution might be slightly higher due to the additional infrastructure involved.

Do I need to purchase additional features like advanced management for everyone?

No, Sprinkle's add-on features, like advanced security and management tools, can be purchased and applied to specific user groups or projects within your organization, enabling you to optimize costs and manage access effectively.

Can I purchase additional features for smaller teams with less than a certain user count?

Yes, Sprinkle offers flexible options for smaller teams. You can either purchase individual add-on licenses or explore tiered plans that include access to specific features.

contact us to discuss options tailored to your team size and requirements.
